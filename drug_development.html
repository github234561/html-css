Drug discovery and development

Therapeutic areas include

Drug Discovery

This is the first phase and includes identifying the lead compound(chemical/biological compound) which fits in the target and cures a particular disease.

Pre-Clinical Trials

This phase includes testing the safety and efficacy on animals.

Clinical trials

Clinical trials in turn include three phases
Phase 1
Conducted on healthy volunteers
Phase 2
Conducted on limited number of patients (hundreds in number)
Phase 3
Conducted on larger number of patients from across the globe.
Note that in each of the above phases safety and efficacy are the primary concerns.
